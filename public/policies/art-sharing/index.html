Art Sharing

You are always welcome to share any public link on this site to your social media. This is not permission to use the image file(s) of original artworks for your own benefit, display, sale, etc.

If you want to purchase a license to use an artwork, please contact Jess Brown and obtain written permission.

Ethical Use of Artwork

The purpose of this site as well as any original artwork featured here is to elevate, promote, and secure the dignity of humanity. Original artwork will not be associated with or used by any person or organization that refuses to respect these values entirely. Racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, and any other form of discrimination are in direct conflict with this purpose and will not be tolerated.

If a license is purchased and/or permission to use the work is obtained, you are still expected to use the work properly according to this ethics statement.
